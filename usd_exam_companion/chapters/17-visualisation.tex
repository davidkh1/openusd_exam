\CheatSheetTitle
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{UsdShade}: shading networks as scene description.
  \item \textbf{Material binding}: usually via \texttt{material:binding} relationship.
  \item \textbf{GeomSubset}: bind per-face materials without splitting meshes.
  \item \textbf{Primvar-driven shading}: wire primvar reader into UsdPreviewSurface inputs.
\end{itemize}
